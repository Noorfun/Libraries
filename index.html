Our Community Story

What serves as an essential community hub or social infrastructure that provides free access to information, education, and resources?
The answer is libraries!

In Fairfax County, countless students and people of all ages go to libraries to work, socialize, study, and take advantage of the resources that libraries offer. Beyond reading materials, libraries play an important role in our community as places to learn, explore, and connect.
